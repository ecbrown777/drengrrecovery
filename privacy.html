Privacy Policy

Drengr Recovery Solutions LLC · Last updated: June 29, 2026

Drengr Recovery Solutions LLC ("Drengr," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our website or submitting information through our forms, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, mailing address, telephone number, and details about a potential surplus-funds claim, when you contact us or submit a form. We may also collect basic technical data (such as IP address and browser type) through standard website analytics.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to inquiries, evaluate and process surplus-funds recovery claims, communicate with you about your case, and meet legal and regulatory obligations. We do not sell your personal information.

SMS / Text Messaging

No mobile information is shared with third parties for marketing. Mobile phone numbers and SMS/text-messaging consent are collected solely for the purpose of communicating with you. We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number or SMS consent with any third parties or affiliates for their marketing or promotional purposes. Text-messaging originator opt-in data and consent are never shared with third parties except as required to deliver the messaging service itself (for example, our messaging platform provider).

If you opt in to receive text messages from Drengr Recovery Solutions LLC, message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time, or reply HELP for assistance. See our Terms of Service & Messaging Terms for full details.

How We Share Information

We may share information with service providers who help us operate (such as document, hosting, and communications vendors), with attorneys or government agencies as needed to process a claim, and where required by law. We do not sell personal information, and we never share SMS consent or mobile data for third-party marketing.

Data Retention & Security

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described here or as required by law, and we use reasonable safeguards to protect it. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your Choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us. You may opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP. You may opt out of email communications using the unsubscribe link or by contacting us directly.
